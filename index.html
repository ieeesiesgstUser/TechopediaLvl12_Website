Frequently Asked Questions

What is Techopedia Lvl 12?

Techopedia Level 12 marks the twelfth edition of IEEE SIES GST's annual technical fest

What does the theme 'PIXELS' imply?

The theme revolves around the exciting world of gaming.

What are the benefits of participating in these events?

Participation increases your chances of winning cash prizes, coupons, goodies, national-level certificates, the prestigious title of "Techopedia Level 12 Winner," and more!

What is the registration fee?

The Registration Fee is Rs. 80 for a team of two members in Squabble, Inquisitive & Eureka, and Rs. 180 for a team of three in Warlash.

Is On-day registration open?

On-day registrations is available for WarLash! AND Ensure you register before the closing date for incredible fun at Techopedia Level 12.
